About Us

The Nigerian association of computer science students OAU branch is an association of vibrant students of the department of computer science and engineering,OAU,aimed at improving the technical and social well being of her members and bridge the gap between the academic environment and computer industry.

It comprises of students ranging from part 1 to part 5. We back backed up by 5 different staff advisers which see to and aid the operations of the association as well as provide adviser on matters beyond our control

The associations main project and activity are the Inn hub and Nacoss week respectively.
